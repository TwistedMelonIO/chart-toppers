# v5.7.0 Release Notes — Pack content alignment + QA sign-off

**Released: May 1, 2026**

## What's New

### Round 4 parity across all packs
- **Summary**: All three packs now have exactly 35 tracks in Round 4 — Final Showdown.
- **Details**: Pack 1 (UK/USA/Germany) gained 5 tracks moved over from Pack 2 (Africa, Lemon Tree, Smells Like Teen Spirit, Hotel California, What a Wonderful World), renumbered to the `PACK_A_*` convention. Pack 2 trimmed those 5. Pack 3 (Teens) lost 4 Retro Classics tracks (Coldplay, White Stripes, Radiohead, Beck). All R4 cues re-cued sequentially `R4T1`–`R4T35`.
- **Impact**: Round 4 plays the same length regardless of pack selection.

### Pack 1 Round 1 expanded to master PDF
- **Summary**: Pack 1 — UK / USA / Germany Round 1 now carries all 57 song-pairs (114 cues) from the master track sheet.
- **Details**: Three genres at 19 song-pairs each — `70's, 80's & 90's`, `BOY BANDS`, `COUNTRY HITS`. New tracks resolve from the original `ROUND 1 - NAME IN 5/` source folder; amended slots use the `NEW AMMENDS/ROUND 1/` REVEAL-style filenames.
- **Impact**: Bigger Round 1 pool for shuffling, and the genre labels now match the operator's master sheet.

### Pack 3 — Sunmi removed
- Pack 3 R1 dropped Sunmi — Gashina (K Pop) at slot 52; mp3 not available. Round 1 re-cued to 55 song-pairs (110 cues).

## Bug Fixes

- **Unicode-normalised pack filenames**: 13 R1/R2/R4 entries across Pack 2 and Pack 3 had NFC/NFD mismatch with the actual mp3 filenames on the show volume (Bamboléo, Tití Me Preguntó, Sarà perché ti amo, Beyoncé, Rosé, Alejandro Fernández, etc.). Caused QLab to silently fall back to the previous cue's file. JSON entries now match the on-disk encoding.
- **R4 file resolution gaps**: 5 mp3s renamed during the Pack 2 → Pack 1 R4 transfer existed only in `Downloads/`. Copied to the show audio volume so QLab no longer plays a stale fallback file.
- **Mama's Don't Let Your Babies (Willie Nelson & Waylon Jennings)** mp3 was missing from the show volume — copied across; QLab now targets the correct file.

## Technical Changes

- `QA_BATCH_SIZES['4']` reduced from 40 → 35 to match Round 4's actual track count. QA UI now reads `Tracks 1-35 of 35` instead of `1-40 of 35`.
- `genreShrinkThresholds['5520x1080']` raised from 13 → 17 (config in `pack-settings.json`) so `70's, 80's & 90's` (17 chars) renders at the full 80pt instead of being auto-shrunk to 61pt.
- Added `data/packs/_backup_*` snapshots before each destructive edit (R1 expansion, R4 re-cueing).
